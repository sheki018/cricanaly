A player's physical fitness plays a crucial role during the match. Choosing a wrong player may bring defeat to the team and also affect the player's physical strength. Because if a player keeps pushing the limits of his body constantly, it can expose him to fatigue, stress, fracture, ligament tear and also mental illness.

As they say, "Prevention is better than cure" making good decisions prior to a match benefits both the team collectively and the player individually. By providing player's current data, decision is made by the model if a player can play in the current match or not.

By doing so, the performance of a player can be optimized by reducing the risk of injury, detecting excessive fatigue and constantly adapting rest, recovery, training and competition loads, based on the players' individual physical and psychological wellness, fitness and health.

Severity of an injury is measured in 5 levels.

  1. Small scratch and cuts, finger nail torn, finger concussion
  2. Muscle spasm,low back pain(less severity), stiff back, split webing
  3. Abdominal side strain, ankle sprain, hamstring strain, thowers elbow
  4. Hamstring tear, stress facture, contusion, getting hit in sensitive area, any knee related injury
  5. Facture ( any where in the body),dislocation
