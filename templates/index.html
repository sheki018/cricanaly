Our Solution

An athlete in his/her prime can win a gold medal in Olympics or even win games single handedly for their team. But pushing the limits of his /her body constantly can expose him/her to fatigue, stress, fracture, ligament tear and also mental illness. That's why workload management is very important. 

CricAnaly optimizes the performance of a player by

Reducing the risk of injury

Detecting excessive fatigue

Identifying its causes

Constantly adapting rest, recovery, training and competition loads, based on the athletes' individual physical and psychological wellness, fitness and health

Services

All the necessary factors are computed and the model can predict when a player needs a break or guidance or practice without any manual calculations which can save time and manpower. It can thereby increase the efficiency of a player.

Fitness Calculator

Considering player's past and present activities, decisions are made if a player is fit to play or not in the current match.

Mental Health

All players have mental & health problems. Considering this and planning accordingly will drastically increase the score of the player in a match.
